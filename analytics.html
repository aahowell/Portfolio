Visual Analytics: Domestic and Sexual Violence Data

Role: Project Lead, Developer

About: This interactive was created for INLS690 at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Students were challenged with developing a prototype visual analytics system designed to solve a problem outlined in their respective project proposals. The purpose of this project was to provide a visual analysis of North Carolina's county-level domestic violence and sexual assault advocacy programs/agency data. Read our team's project proposal here.

Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, D3, Simple Statistics
