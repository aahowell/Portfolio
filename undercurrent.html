Undercurrent

Role: Assistant Creative Director, Project Designer

About: In March 2016, a team of 25 photojournalism and interactive/design students in the UNC School of Media and Journalism came together to create a multimedia documentary. The group travelled to Bocas Del Toro, Panama to document stories of change in the environment and culture. As a member of the design/development team, I focused on the visual presentation and functionality of all video stories, 360 videos, and interactive graphics. As an assistant to the art director, I created responsive website design concepts that included color palettes, font styles, and site organization ideas.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, AfterEffects, Photoshop
