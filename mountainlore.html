In Her Hands

Role: Videographer | Editor

About: This video was created as part of the Carolina Photojournalism Workshop (CPJW), an experience in which students travel to a unique part of North Carolina to produce a documentary website. May 2016, UNC School of Media and Journalism students produced “Mountain Lore”, a documentary consisting of 13 short films, that tells the stories of the people and culture of Jackson County, North Carolina. As a videographer, I contacted and spent days getting to know my subject. I shot and edited this 3 minute video to tell her story. After finding peace among the mountains of Sylva, N.C., Pat Mikula molds the world on a pottery wheel.

Tools: Adobe Premiere Pro
