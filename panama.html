In Her Hands

Role: Co-Animator and Designer

About: The motion graphic 'Panama Disease: A Global Issue' was created as part of the documentary project Undercurrent. It is featured in Chapter 4 of the documentary, Built With Bananas. The purpose of the motion graphic is to explain what Panama Disease is, where it came from, and its potential future impact on the banana industry. The project required research, script writing, storyboarding, audio recording, asset creation, animation, and editing.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, AfterEffects, Photoshop
